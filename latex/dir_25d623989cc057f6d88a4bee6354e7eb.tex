\item {\ttfamily subtype}\+: The subtype of the media type (always lower case). Example\+: `\textquotesingle{}svg'\`{}
\item {\ttfamily suffix}\+: The suffix of the media type (always lower case). Example\+: `\textquotesingle{}xml'\`{}
