{\itshape regenerate-\/unicode-\/properties} is a collection of \href{https://github.com/mathiasbynens/regenerate}{\texttt{ Regenerate}} sets for \href{https://github.com/tc39/proposal-regexp-unicode-property-escapes}{\texttt{ various Unicode properties}}.\hypertarget{README.md_autotoc_md17355}{}\doxysubsection{\texorpdfstring{Installation}{Installation}}\label{README.md_autotoc_md17355}

\begin{DoxyCode}{0}
\DoxyCodeLine{\$\ npm\ install\ regenerate-\/unicode-\/properties}

\end{DoxyCode}
