Wrap words to the specified column width.\hypertarget{readme.md_autotoc_md986}{}\doxysubsubsubsection{\texorpdfstring{string}{string}}\label{readme.md_autotoc_md986}
Type\+: {\ttfamily string}

String with ANSI escape codes. Like one styled by \href{https://github.com/chalk/chalk}{\texttt{ {\ttfamily chalk}}}. Newline characters will be normalized to {\ttfamily \textbackslash{}n}.\hypertarget{readme.md_autotoc_md987}{}\doxysubsubsubsection{\texorpdfstring{columns}{columns}}\label{readme.md_autotoc_md987}

Number of columns to wrap the text to.\hypertarget{readme.md_autotoc_md988}{}\doxysubsubsubsection{\texorpdfstring{options}{options}}\label{readme.md_autotoc_md988}
Type\+: {\ttfamily object}\hypertarget{readme.md_autotoc_md989}{}\doxysubsubsubsubsection{\texorpdfstring{hard}{hard}}\label{readme.md_autotoc_md989}
Type\+: {\ttfamily boolean}\textbackslash{} Default\+: {\ttfamily false}

By default the wrap is soft, meaning long words may extend past the column width. Setting this to {\ttfamily true} will make it hard wrap at the column width.\hypertarget{readme.md_autotoc_md990}{}\doxysubsubsubsubsection{\texorpdfstring{word\+Wrap}{word\+Wrap}}\label{readme.md_autotoc_md990}

By default, an attempt is made to split words at spaces, ensuring that they don\textquotesingle{}t extend past the configured columns.
